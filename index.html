UBC Solar Bench Project


As the Software Lead in Innovation, Design, Sustain club, I was responsible for leading a team of Software Engineers in the Solar Bench project. Our goal was to install benches on the UBC campus that are powered by the sun and provide a charging station to students. In addition to this, the bench has a screen that shows students information about campus news. Python, Grafana, and Google Cloud Platform are used to monitor the bench and alert the team in case of outrages.

IUsta

IUsta was the personal project I developed and deployed in my hometown, Baku. The app's premise was to connect customers and repairers. Repairers would sign up and choose their expertise and set their own prices. In case of a maintenance issue, customers could open the app and choose any free repairer to come to their house. The app had an integrated rating and map system. I built the app using Flutter and Firebase for back end. You can check the app on App Store and PlayStore

CareTaker Buddy

CareTaker Buddy was a team project we developed during BCHacks Hackathon, and won. The app consists of two parts CareTaker and SeniorBuddy. With the help of our app, senior citizens get reminders to take their medicine, stay mentally and physically active, and do other assignments. In case of an emergency, senior citizens can easily notify their caretakers through the app. The app also included a personalized chatbot to help seniors.GitHub
